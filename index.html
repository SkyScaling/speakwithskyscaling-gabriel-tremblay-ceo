Gabriel Tremblay

Founder & CEO
Quebec City, Canada

Hi, I'm Gabriel, founder of Sky Scaling, and I come from the beautiful city of Quebec City, Canada. My journey into marketing and the financial world is not exactly what you would call "traditional."

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Finance from Bishop's University. Ironically, while I was studying finance, I was already knee deep in marketing. I had this tug of war going on: my diploma said finance, but my passion screamed marketing. Instead of picking one, I combined them and decided to build something of my own.

That is how Sky Scaling was born. I wanted to help professionals in the financial industry stand out in a digital world. And here is the funny part. While I thought I would be helping everyone, it seemed like every single person booking calls with me turned out to be an insurance agent. I quickly realized the insurance industry had a huge need for modern marketing, support, and real guidance. So I doubled down, went all in, and never looked back.

Now I spend my time helping insurance agents grow, attract more clients and recruits, and build systems that let them scale without burning out. My focus is not just on numbers. It's on giving agents the confidence to thrive in an industry that is often misunderstood and sometimes overlooked.

Outside of work, I like to keep things lighthearted and I believe business does not always have to be stiff or boring. After all, if you cannot laugh while building something great, what is the point?

With me, you will find someone who mixes financial know how with marketing creativity, a little bit of structure, a little bit of chaos, and a whole lot of dedication to helping agents win.
